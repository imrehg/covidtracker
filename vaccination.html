Unfortunately due to the way the graphs on this page are generated, they don't look great on smaller screens. You may have better luck rotating your device to a landscape orientation.

The plot below shows the case rate for each local authority against its vaccine rollout. This plot combines the first and second doses by assuming 40% of the protection 14 days after the first dose, and the remaining 60% protection 14 days after the second dose. The "tails" show the trajectory over the past 7 days.

Data in time series plots from before January 10 is based on interpolated weekly numbers by vaccination date. After that, numbers are daily by report date.
